### Subjuntivo [ˌsu.bi.ʒũˈtʃi.vu] #### e as orações subordinadas
**subordinate clause**: a clause that forms part of a sentence and is dependent on (or forms part of) another clause, phrase, or sentence element. *The Oxford Dictionary of English Grammar* (1 rev. ed.)
I was surprised, *because it was so unexpected*.
It was odd *that he didn’t telephone*. I was surprised *by what you said*.
The news (*that*) *you gave us is very odd*.
